## 1. Police Station (Reporting)
**Scenario #52**
**Save as:** `assets/immersion-scenarios/emergency/police-station-reporting.png`

**Prompt:**
```
Illustrate a home repair visit with plumber or electrician. Show a tradesperson (diverse, work uniform, tool belt) explaining a repair issue (pointing at pipes or electrical panel) to a homeowner (casual home clothes, listening attentively). Setting: Home interior - bathroom, kitchen, or utility room with visible repair area, toolbox, ladder. Color palette: Utility blue, safety orange, home neutrals. Mood: Problem-solving, practical, professional home service. Art style: Practical flat illustration, home repair aesthetic. 1200x800px.
```
